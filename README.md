# Vitamin A Guide

A comprehensive guide to Vitamin A, focusing on its critical roles in skin health and vision, along with dietary sources, benefits, deficiency symptoms, and supplementation risks.

## Importance of Vitamin A

Vitamin A is essential for maintaining healthy vision, particularly in low-light conditions, and for supporting skin health by promoting cell growth and differentiation. It also plays a role in immune function and reproduction.
